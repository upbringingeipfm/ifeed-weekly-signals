Signal 01 of 09 · AI Governance · Draft Commission guidelines on classification of high-risk AI systems

High-risk AI classification is becoming operating architecture.

The European Commission draft high-risk AI classification source gives iFeed a clear governance signal: classification cannot sit as a vague legal label. It needs an operating record that connects intended purpose, product or safety-component relationship, source interpretation, reviewer, and limitation. For iFeed, the important move is not to overstate the consultation as final guidance, but to turn it into a controlled decision workflow.
